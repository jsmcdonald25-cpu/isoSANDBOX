ISO/GRADING Technology
The Most Transparent
Grading System Ever Built.

Every other grading company hands you a number and tells you to trust them. GrailISO shows you exactly how we got there — AI analysis, professional review, and a public grading scale anyone can read.

🤖
AI Analysis First

Every card is scanned at high resolution and analyzed by our grading AI. Surface wear, print defects, centering deviation, corner sharpness, and edge integrity — all measured precisely, zero subjectivity.

👁️
Professional Review

A certified GrailISO grader reviews every AI result before a grade is issued. They confirm, adjust, or flag for re-scan. AI speeds the process — a professional signs off on every result.

📖
Public Grading Scale

Our grading rubric is published in full. Every sub-category weight, definition, and edge case — open to the public. You know exactly how your card will be evaluated before you submit it.
