AUTHENTICITY VERIFICATION

Every piece is scanned on upload. AI detects duplicates, stolen work, and stock image elements. Clean pieces earn the Authentic badge before listing goes live.

1
Artist Uploads
Piece submitted with front/back photos, medium, dimensions, and player/subject details.
2
AI Scans for Originality
System checks for duplicate uploads, known stock imagery, and digital reproduction markers.
3
Badge Applied — Listing Goes Live
Flagged pieces go to manual admin review. Confirmed fraud: escrow returned to buyer, seller CC charged return cost.

HOW IT WORKS
🎨
Artist Fee
$0

Artists never pay anything to list, sell, or commission work on ISO/ARTS. Ever. The only exception is confirmed fraud — seller is charged the cost to return funds to the buyer.

🎓
Buyer Premium
10%

Buyers pay a 10% premium on every purchase. 100% of that amount goes to the ISO/ARTS Scholarship Fund — administered by our nonprofit partner, not GrailISO.

🏫
Monthly Donation
100%

Every month, accumulated scholarship funds are donated to the contest winner's nominated school, art program, or nonprofit of their choice.
